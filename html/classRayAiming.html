Detailed Description

RayAiming implements rayaiming :)

RayAiming is the process to find (for a given start position of a ray) the right direction to later pass through another point (typically given in the plane of the (aperture) stop). More or less intelligent algorithms for that are possible, also user assistance is possible. And since rayaiming is necessary typically for every pass during optimization it is also beneficial to do this quite fast... Lots of possibilities for the future. Also for subclasses.
